Ghee

Barani Ghee is the must-buy product for every household for its purest quality and the spellbound smell and taste. The clarified butter is made of the best milk from the Indian cows which are fed with grass.Barani Ghee is rich with nutty flavor and the high-smoke point which makes it perfect for sautéing, searing, frying and roasting. Ghee is rich with antioxidants and acts as an aid in the absorption of vitamins and minerals from other foods and strengthens the immune system. Barani Ghee is the rich source of vitamin A, D, K & E and also is a best friend of your kitchen who brings out the best version of any dish..
