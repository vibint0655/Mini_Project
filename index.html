BARANI GHEE BUTTER

Nothing like tasty after you get our item

Ghee is a clarified butter variant popular in Middle Eastern and Indian cuisines. It's created from cow's milk butter that has been cooked at a low temperature until the water evaporates, leaving only the milk solids. Then, if necessary, the solids are skimmed or filtered.
